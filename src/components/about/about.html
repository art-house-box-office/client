About Art House Box Office

This application is designed for independent cinema owners to track their own movie sales, and compare box office numbers against national trends as other cinema owners enter their data.

As the manager of an independent movie theater, you know that making programming decisions is both an art and a science. You are using your experience to balance choices that excite your audience and films that fulfill your mission as a theater.

This site is designed as a tool for making more informed programing choices. You, along with scores of other independent cinema owners, enter your box office data and by doing so you can compare the trends among your own screenings and compare them with national trends among your peers.

Do Action genres perform better as matinees or late nights?

You're thinking about presenting an Akira Kurosawa retrospective, but first you want to see some typical attendance numbers for this director's films.

How do your late night horror shows compare with the national trends?

What genres are the most profitable on Wednesdays?

These are the questions that Art House Box Office can help answer. Enjoy!

This application was created using Angular, hosted on Firebase, with the API running on Node.js hosted by Heroku backed by a MongoDB database provided by mLab. Thanks to all the open-source developers that have put in all the effort to make these projects possible so that we can make awesome projects!
